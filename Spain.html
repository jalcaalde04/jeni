what is Spain

Spain is a fascinating country located on the Iberian Peninsula, southwest of Europe, which combines a rich ancient history with a vibrant modern culture. It is known for its geographical diversity, from Mediterranean beaches to the Pyrenees mountains, passing through extensive plains and green forests in the north.
Spanish culture is a mosaic of traditions, with 17 autonomous communities that maintain their own customs, languages and unique cultural expressions. The country is world famous for its gastronomy, which includes paella, tapas and exquisite wines, as well as for cultural events such as flamenco, bullfights and popular festivals such as La Tomatina or Sanfermines.
The Spanish are known for their open and sociable nature, their Mediterranean lifestyle and their late lunch and dinner times. The country is also a major tourist destination, with historic cities such as Barcelona, Madrid, Seville and Granada, home to architectural gems ranging from ancient mosques and Gothic cathedrals to modernist works by Gaudi
